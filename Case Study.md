## The Problem With Most Wealth Management Brands

Most finance sites look like they were designed by someone who's never actually worried about money.

Dark navy. Gold accents. Stock photo of a handshake. Copy that says "we put clients first" and means absolutely nothing.

And here's the thing - that coldness isn't accidental.

Most wealth management firms DESIGN for authority. Not for trust. Not for the person sitting across the table wondering if they can hand over their life savings to someone who actually gives a damn.

Solis was built to fix that.

Warm but not weak.
Premium but not cold.
Personal but not unprofessional.

## The Brand System

Warm Stone. That's the whole palette in two words.

| Role | Token | Hex |
|------|-------|-----|
| Background | Off White | `#FAF8F4` |
| Background Tint | Off White - 20% | `#FAF8F4` at 10% |
| Card / Surface | Container White | `#FAF6E8` |
| Text / Dark BG | Off Black | `#1E1A14` |
| Dark BG Tint | Off Black - 20% | `#1E1A14` at 20% |
| Body Text | Taupe | `#7A7260` |
| Secondary Text | Light Taupe | `#A89880` |
| Primary / Accent | Orange | `#E8500A` |

Cream backgrounds. Espresso darks. Burnt orange accent.

Nothing cold. Nothing corporate. Every color earns its place by making the page feel like a conversation - not a pitch deck.

**Typography:**

| Role | Font |
|------|------|
| H1 | `Archivo` Bold - 48px / 1.1 - tight and authoritative without being aggressive |
| H2-H6 | `Archivo` Bold - 40px down to 19px / 1.4 - consistent warmth across all sizes |
| Body | `Archivo` Regular - 16px / 1.4 - readable, human, never sterile |
| Body Small | `Archivo` Regular - 13px / 1.4 - secondary copy and captions |
| Body Small Bold | `Archivo` Semibold - 13px - Orange - accent labels and eyebrows |
| Button | `Archivo` Regular - 16px / 1.2 - Off White - clear and confident |

One font family. THAT'S IT. 🙃

Archivo does everything - from the 48px hero headline to the 13px caption. No mixing, no handoff confusion, no "which font goes here" decisions. The range within Archivo handles the whole system.

**Icons:** Phosphor Icons - Duotone weight throughout.

Duotone Phosphor in burnt orange on dark cards - hits differently. Functional, premium, on-brand without trying too hard.

### Navigation

### Hero

Center-aligned. Big Archivo Black headline. One orange CTA. Video block below that peeks into the fold.

The headline does the emotional work first. The sub-copy follows with the rational. Trust strip below with press logos keeps it grounded.

### Philosophy / About

> *"We built warmer wealth management"*

Split layout. Headline and copy left. Three-slide carousel right.

Each slide is a dark UI card with a real image, a Phosphor icon, a label, heading, and one-liner. Three pillars:

- **Our Foundation** - Built on listening first
- **Our Belief** - Your wealth is personal. We treat it that way
- **Our Promise** - We're here for the long run

The carousel format keeps it tight. The UI card style matches the rest of the system.

---

### Features Bento Grid

Dark espresso section. Bento grid layout. Custom UI cards for each cell.

Four cells - one advisor card, performance chart, life goals card, and an orange process flow card on the right. The orange cell is the visual anchor. Every card is a real functional UI mockup - no abstract shapes, no stock illustrations.

## The Copy

Yeppp - every single word on this site was written from scratch. 🙃

Not "placeholder text replaced with real words."

Actually written. With a brand voice. With intention.

With the specific kind of person who lands on this page in mind.

The voice rule for Solis was simple: The Human Partner.

Warm. Personal. First-person plural. Leads with the emotional truth before the rational fact. Uses "you" and "we" throughout. Acknowledges that money is emotional, not just mathematical. Reads like a letter from someone who actually gives a damn.

No jargon. No "synergies." No "wealth solutions." No corporate speak.

Just honest copy that makes someone feel safe enough to pick up the phone.

---

## The Images

No stock photos. No AI silhouettes. No generic handshake imagery.

Every visual on this site is a custom dark-mode UI card - designed to feel like a real product screen. Portfolio dashboards, session timelines, tax savings breakdowns, estate flow diagrams, family tree structures.

The direction was simple: if it looks like a screenshot from an actual product, it belongs. If it looks like a decoration, it doesn't.
